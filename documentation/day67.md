# Mock Demo Day – Zecpath AI Evaluation

## Objective
To simulate live stakeholder presentation conditions, test timing limits, evaluate slide structures, and document key Q&A scenarios.

## Key Features
* **Q&A Database**: Tracks typical recruiter questions (concerning bias removal, system scale, and security) and designs concise answers.
* **Timing calibrations**: Sets strict quotas (Slide deck ~11 mins, live walkthough ~10 mins, questions ~4 mins) to fit within 25 minutes.
* **Refined Presentation strategy**: Focuses on slower verbal deliveries, storytelling structures, and simplifying technical topologies.

---

## Advantages
* **Reduces speaker anxiety**: Pre-scripted responses ensure smooth slide transitions.
* **Polishes system explanations**: Minimizes technical jargon in favor of business outcome statements.

## Limitations
* Simulated mock testing does not capture live evaluator follow-up questions.

## Future Improvements
* Record dry-run sessions to evaluate verbal pace.
* Seek evaluation reviews from cross-department advisors.
