# HR Interview AI – Final System Report

## Objective
Deliver a production-ready AI-powered HR interview system.

## System Capabilities
* **Automated HR interviews**: Generates questions, reads responses, and processes follow-ups automatically.
* **Real-time evaluation**: Extracts candidate skills, identifies behavioral intent, and normalizes scores.
* **Behavioral analysis**: Monitors candidate confidence, stress signals, and logical consistency.
* **Hiring recommendations**: Incorporates ATS parsing, Screening rounds, and HR feedback into a unified score.

## Business Impact
* **Reduces recruiter workload**: Automates initial screening stages, saving up to 80% recruiter time.
* **Speeds up hiring process**: Compresses multi-week evaluation rounds into single-day pipelines.
* **Improves decision consistency**: Minimizes unconscious hiring bias through structured scoring engines.
