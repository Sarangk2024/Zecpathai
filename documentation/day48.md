# Behavioral AI Design – Zecpath

## Objective
To enhance hiring decisions by analyzing candidate focus, engagement, and distraction levels using non-invasive camera and interaction metadata.

## Key Features
* **Non-invasive analysis**: Processing interaction feeds client-side to generate metadata signals without storing raw video.
* **Real-time signal processing**: Instant weighting of focus and movement signals.
* **Behavioral scoring**: Transparent metrics mapping signals to specific focus ranges.
* **Recruiter insights**: Clear reports indicating overall candidate attentiveness.

## Advantages
* **Improves candidate evaluation**: Adds structural context regarding attentiveness.
* **Adds behavioral intelligence**: Detects when candidates struggle to maintain engagement.
* **Non-intrusive design**: Focuses strictly on metadata rather than full-scale emotional profiling.

## Limitations
* No deep facial feature emotional classification (e.g. tracking micro-expressions).
* Depends heavily on user webcam frames and lighting quality.

## Future Improvements
* AI-based nervous hand gesture classifiers.
* Custom real-time feedback cues encouraging candidates to adjust camera angles.
* Deep learning eye gaze calibration engines.

---

## Ethical Considerations
* **No facial recognition storage**: Biometric parameters mapping identities are never created or kept.
* **No identity tracking**: Process is anonymous across candidates.
* **Only behavioral metadata stored**: RAW webcam streaming files are discarded immediately.
* **Candidate consent required**: Candidates must explicitly consent before camera trackers initialize.
