# Next-Gen AI Hiring Innovations – Zecpath

## 1. AI Video Intelligence System
* **Eye Tracking**: Analyzes gaze distributions to ensure the candidate remains focused on the screen, minimizing external distractions or screen reading flags.
* **Facial Engagement**: Tracks micro-expressions to evaluate candidate enthusiasm, interest, and engagement levels.
* **Posture Detection**: Ensures proper positioning within the camera view to avoid audio/video clipping.

---

## 2. Emotion Detection Engine
* **Vocal Stress Metrics**: Identifies spikes in candidate nervousness or hesitation by tracking speech delays.
* **Pitch Analysis**: Detects tone shifts, identifying confidence indicators during self-introductions and project walkthroughs.

---

## 3. Real-Time AI Feedback Loops
* **Adaptive Interviewing**: Modifies question difficulties dynamically during technical interviews based on current performance.
* **Guidance Prompts**: Displays indicators on the dashboard if candidate speech pace falls outside acceptable ranges.

---

## 4. AI Coaching Modules
Provides candidates with:
* Detailed scoring breakdowns across core competencies (communication, technical skills, machine tests).
* Specific study path recommendations (e.g. "Review database index designs").
* Actionable presentation adjustments (e.g. "Try to reduce vocal pauses during introductions").
