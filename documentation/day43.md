# Ethics & Compliance – Zecpath AI

## Objective
To ensure responsible AI usage in hiring decisions.

## Key Features
* **Fairness-focused scoring**: Normalized score parameters and candidate weight distributions without demographic bias.
* **Transparent decision-making**: Provides per-round explainable scores to recruiters.
* **Privacy protection**: Automatic candidate PII masking during evaluations.
* **Regulatory alignment**: Automated 90-day retention checks and right to erasure compliance flows.

## Advantages
* **Builds trust with candidates**: Clear upfront consent policy makes the process transparent.
* **Reduces legal risks**: Masking and data minimization principles protect candidate civil rights.
* **Ensures ethical hiring**: Hiring decisions are driven strictly by candidate capabilities and experience.

## Limitations
* Partial GDPR compliance.
* Lacks real-time bias detection dashboards.

## Future Improvements
* Real-time automated bias detection and warning indicators.
* Third-party AI fairness and diversity audits.
* Full-scale explainability dashboard interface.
