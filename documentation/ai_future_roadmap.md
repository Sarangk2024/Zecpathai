# Zecpath AI Future Roadmap (2026–2028)

## Phase 1: Intelligence Enhancement (0–6 Months)
* **LLM Integration**: Transition from rule-based keyword extraction to large language models for parsing resumes and technical scoring.
* **Accuracy Calibration**: Continual fine-tuning of staging weights against real human recruiter override rates.
* **Advanced Behavioral Processing**: Integrate hesitation and stress signals directly into conversational scoring pipelines.

## Phase 2: Advanced AI Features (6–12 Months)
* **AI Video Analysis**: Analyze eye movements, head orientation, and facial gestures to evaluate attentiveness.
* **Voice Emotion Detection**: Monitor vocal pitch and speech delivery variations.
* **Real-time UI Cues**: Prompt candidates when they look away or move out of the camera view.

## Phase 3: Platform Intelligence (12–18 Months)
* **AI Coaching System**: Suggest resume enhancements or specific skill paths for candidates based on their scoring.
* **Interview Analytics Dashboards**: Recruiter dashboard metrics mapping overall pipeline conversions and skill distributions.
* **Candidate Benchmarking**: Index applicant performances against global roles averages.

## Phase 4: Enterprise Scale AI (18–24 Months)
* **Multi-Language Support**: Support local dialect translations and voice transcriptions.
* **Personalized Interrogators**: Build customized conversational style agents matching corporate cultural values.
* **Zero-Trust Security**: Ensure complete end-to-end data encryption of transcripts and scores at the edge.
