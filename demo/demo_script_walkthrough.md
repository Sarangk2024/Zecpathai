### Slide 1 – Introduction
*"Good morning stakeholders and evaluators. Today, I am proud to present Zecpath AI, our end-to-end intelligent hiring engine designed to optimize recruitment timelines."*

---

### Slide 2 – The Problem
*"Currently, high-volume recruitment is bottlenecked by manual resume screening, biased filtering, and slow coordination. Recruitment teams waste hundreds of hours screening unqualified candidates."*

---

### Slide 3 – The Solution
*"Zecpath AI automates this entire pipeline. We extract profiles, run semantic ATS matches, schedule automated screening calls, evaluate communication confidence, check coding correctness in sandbox tests, and output consistent hiring recommendations."*

---

### Slide 4 – Walkthrough Demo Cues
*"Let’s run our pipeline simulation. When a resume is submitted, the parsing engine extracts skills and years of experience. Next, the eligibility engine filters candidate qualifications. During the interview, our NLP checks candidate answers to detect off-topic responses, while visual trackers evaluate gaze stability. Finally, the aggregator merges these checks into a structured recruiter scorecard."*

---

### Slide 5 – Closing & Business Value
