# Zecpath AI Hiring System – Product Demo Presentation Deck

## Slide 1: Title Slide
* **Title**: Zecpath AI Hiring Platform
* **Sub-title**: End-to-End Intelligent Recruitment & Screening System
* **Speaker**: Zecpath AI Core Engineering team

---

## Slide 2: Problem Statement
Modern recruitment processes suffer from:
* **High Latency**: Manual resume screening and scheduling take days.
* **Demographic Bias**: Unconscious bias affects interview screening choices.
* **High Cost & Low Scale**: HR teams struggle to evaluate thousands of candidate files.

---

## Slide 3: Our AI Solution
Zecpath AI automates the recruitment workflow:
* **ATS Scoring**: Semantic profiling and requirements checking.
* **Outbound Screening**: Automated conversational calls verifying availability and parameters.
* **HR & Technical Interviews**: Telemetry, voice confidence, and technical scoring modules.
* **Decision AI Engine**: Unified aggregation pipelines mapping final suitability ratings.

---

## Slide 4: System Architecture
* **Frontend**: Recruiter and Applicant portal dashboards.
* **Gateway Layer**: JWT security checks and API routing.
* **Microservices**: Parser, Scorer, Screening, Behavioral, Technical, and Aggregator nodes.
* **Storage**: Encrypted DB storing transcripts, metrics logs, and audit logs.

---

## Slide 5: Business Impact & Outcomes
* **70% Reduction** in total hiring cycle timelines.
* **90% Automation** of high-volume candidate screening tasks.
* **Consistency**: Standardized evaluations remove hiring biases.
