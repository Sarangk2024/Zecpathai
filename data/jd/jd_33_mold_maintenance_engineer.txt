33. Mold Maintenance Engineer

Job Summary  
Handles maintenance, repair, and optimization of molds used in plastic injection and blow 
molding processes.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Perform mold maintenance and repair  
• Inspect cavities, cores, cooling channels, and ejection systems  
• Troubleshoot molding defects related to tooling  
• Conduct mold trials and adjustments  
• Maintain mold history and documentation  
• Improve mo ld performance and cycle time  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of plastic molding processes  
• Mold assembly and troubleshooting skills  
• Precision inspection techniques  
• Problem -solving ability  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Plastics  
Experi ence  
• 3–7 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹30,000 – ₹60,000 per month
