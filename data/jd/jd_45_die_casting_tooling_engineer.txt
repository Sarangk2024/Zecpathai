45. Die Casting Tooling Engineer

Job Summary  
Responsible for tooling used in die casting processes, ensuring efficiency, durability, and 
product quality.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Develop and maintain die casting tooling  
• Support die trials and process opti mization  
• Analyze defects and improve tooling performance  
• Coordinate with production and quality teams  
• Ensure proper cooling and ejection systems  
• Monitor tool life and maintenance  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of die casting processes  
• Tool design and main tenance understanding  
• Problem -solving and defect analysis  
• Material and thermal behavior knowledge  
Qualification  
• B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Production  
Experience  
• 3–7 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹35,000 – ₹70,000 per month
