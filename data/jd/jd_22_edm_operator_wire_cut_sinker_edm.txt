22. EDM Operator (Wire Cut / Sinker EDM)

Job Summary  
Operates EDM machines to produce intricate shapes and high -precision components for 
tooling.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Set up and operate Wire Cut EDM / Sinker EDM machines  
• Prepare electrodes and fixtures  
• Execute cutting programs  
• Monitor machining process and accuracy  
• Maintain EDM machines and consumables  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of EDM processes  
• CNC/EDM programming basics  
• Precision measurement and inspection  
• Understandi ng of tooling materials  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die / CNC / Mechanical  
Experience  
• 2–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹22,000 – ₹40,000 per month
