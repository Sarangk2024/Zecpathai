14. Injection Mold Maker

Job Summary  
Specializes in building molds for plastic injection molding processes.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture injection molds  
• Perform cavity and core finishing  
• Assemble and test molds  
• Support  mold trials and production  
• Maintain cooling and ejection systems  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of injection molding  
• High -precision machining and polishing  
• Mold assembly expertise  
• Troubleshooting skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die / Plastic s Technology  
Experience  
• 4–7 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹28,000 – ₹50,000 per month
