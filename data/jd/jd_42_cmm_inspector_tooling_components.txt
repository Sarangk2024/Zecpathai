42. CMM Inspector (Tooling Components)

Job Summary  
Operates Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) to inspect complex tooling components 
with high precision.  
Key Responsib ilities  
• Program and operate CMM machines  
• Inspect components using 3D measurement techniques  
• Analyze measurement data and generate reports  
• Ensure compliance with tolerances and specifications  
• Maintain CMM equipment and calibration records  
• Support quali ty audits and validations  
Required Skills  
• CMM programming (PC -DMIS, Zeiss Calypso, etc.)  
• Strong GD&T knowledge  
• Precision measurement expertise  
• Analytical skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma / B.E in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 2–6 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹55,000 per month
