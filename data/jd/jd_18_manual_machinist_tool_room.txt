18. Manual Machinist (Tool Room)

Job Summary  
Operates conventional machines to manufacture and repair tooling components with high 
precision.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Operate lathe, milling, drilling, and shaping machines  
• Manufacture tooling parts as per drawings  
• Perform fitting, drilling, and tapping operations  
• Repair worn -out tooling components  
• Maintain machine accuracy and cleanliness  
Required Skills  
• Strong manual machining skills  
• Ability to read engineering drawings  
• Knowledge of cutting tools and speeds/feeds  
• Precision measurement skills  
Qualification  
• ITI in Machinist / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 2–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹18,000 – ₹30,000 per month
