4. Tool Room Assistant

Job Summary  
Provides operational and logistical support in the tool room to ensure smooth workflow and 
tool availability.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Maintain tool inventory and records  
• Issue and receive tool s from production  
• Assist in tool cleaning, storage, and handling  
• Support tool setup and movement  
• Maintain tool room organization  
Required Skills  
• Basic understanding of tools and equipment  
• Organizational and record -keeping skills  
• Basic computer knowle dge (Excel/logbooks)  
• Communication skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma / Any technical certification  
Experience  
• 0–2 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹10,000 – ₹18,000 per month
