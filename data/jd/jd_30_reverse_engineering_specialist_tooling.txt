30. Reverse Engineering Specialist (Tooling)

Job Summary  
Recreates tooling designs from existing physical components using measurement and 
scanning technologies.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Analyze and measure existing tools/compone nts  
• Use 3D scanning and CAD software to recreate designs  
• Prepare accurate models and drawings  
• Improve existing designs where required  
• Support repair, duplication, and redesign of tooling  
• Collaborate with manufacturing teams  
Required Skills  
• Reverse en gineering tools (3D scanning, CAD)  
• Strong measurement and inspection skills  
• CAD modeling expertise  
• Analytical thinking  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 3–6 years  
Salary (India)
