61. Hot Runner System Specialist

Job Summary  
Handles design, maintenance, and optimization of hot runner systems used in injection 
molding processes.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Design and maintain hot runner systems  
• Optimize temperature control and flow  
• Troubleshoot heating and flow issues  
• Support mold trials and production  
• Ensure system efficiency and reliability  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of hot runner systems and injection molding  
• Thermal management expertise  
• Troubleshooting skills  
• Understanding o f plastics processing  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Plastics  
Experience  
• 5–10 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹45,000 – ₹95,000 per month
