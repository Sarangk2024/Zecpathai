68. Tooling Consultant

Job Summary  
Provides expert advice on tooling design, manufacturing, maintenance, and optimization to 
improve pro ductivity and reduce costs.

• Recommend improvements in tool design and performance  
• Support troubleshooting of complex tooling issues

• Guide tool room teams and train staff

• Strong analytical and problem -solving skills  
• Communication and presentation skills  
• Cost optimizatio n and process improvement expertise  
Qualification

Experience
