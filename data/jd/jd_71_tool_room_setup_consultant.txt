71. Tool Room Setup Consultant

Job Summary  
Helps organizations establish or upgrade tool rooms, including layout planning, machine 
selection, and workflow optimization.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Design tool room layout and infrastructure  
• Recommend machines, tools, and equipment  
• Develop workflow and process standards  
• Assist in vendor selection and procurement  
• Train staff and establish best practices  
• Ensure safety and compliance standards  
Required Skills  
• Tool room operations expertise  
• Layout planning and process design  
• Knowledge of machinery and equipment  
• Project planning and execution  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 10–20+ years  
Earnings (India)  
• ₹2,000 – ₹8,000 per hour  
• ₹2,00,000 – ₹10,00,000 per project
