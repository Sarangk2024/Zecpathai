69. Die Design Consultant

Job Summary  
Specializes in consulting for die design, including strip layout, forming analysis, and 
production optimization.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Design and review die concepts and layouts  
• Optimize strip layout for material utilization  
• Conduct feasibility studies  
• Support die trials and debugging  
• Provide technical guidance to design teams  
• Review and approve die designs  
Required Skills  
• Advanced die design expertise  
• Knowledge of sheet met al forming  
• CAD software proficiency (CATIA, NX, SolidWorks)  
• Analytical and troubleshooting skills  
Qualification  
• B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 8–15+ years  
Earnings (India)  
• ₹1,500 – ₹6,000 per hour  
• ₹75,000 – ₹4,00,000 per project
