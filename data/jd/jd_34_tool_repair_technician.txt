34. Tool Repair Technician

Job Summary  
Specializes in repairing damaged or worn tools, dies, and molds to restore functionality and 
extend tool life.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Repair worn or damaged tooling components  
• Perform welding, grinding, and machining repairs  
• Replace inserts and worn parts  
• Reassemble and test repaired tools  
• Maintain repair records  
• Support emergency breakdown repairs  
Required Skills  
• Strong repair and reconditioning skills  
• Knowledge of welding and machining techniques  
• Understanding of tooling materials  
• Precision measurement skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die / Machinist  
Experience  
• 2–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹20,000 – ₹40,000 per month
