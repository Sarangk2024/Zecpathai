57. Micro Tooling Specialist

Job Summary  
Focuses on manufacturing miniature tooling components used in electronics, medical 
devices, and precision industries.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture micro -scale tools and components  
• Use micro -machining and EDM techniques  
• Handle delicate and high -precision materials  
• Perform micro -level inspection and measurement  
• Ensure high accuracy and repeatability  
Required Skills  
• Micro -machining and EDM expertise  
• Knowledge of precision measurement techniques  
• High attention to detail  and steady handling  
• Understanding of advanced materials  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 5–9 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹40,000 – ₹85,000 per month
