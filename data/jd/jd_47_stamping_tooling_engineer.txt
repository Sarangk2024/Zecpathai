47. Stamping Tooling Engineer

Job Summary  
Responsible for tooling used in sheet metal stamping processes, ensuring produ ction 
efficiency and quality.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Develop and maintain stamping tools and dies  
• Support press setup and production runs  
• Analyze defects such as burrs, wrinkles, and cracks  
• Optimize tooling performance and life  
• Coordinate with design and  quality teams  
• Conduct tool trials and improvements  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of stamping and forming processes  
• Tooling design and maintenance understanding  
• Troubleshooting and analytical skills  
• GD&T and inspection knowledge  
Qualification  
• B.E / B.Tec h in Mechanical / Production  
Experience  
• 3–6 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹30,000 – ₹65,000 per month
