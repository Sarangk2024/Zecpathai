39. Mold Quality Engineer

Job Summary  
Responsible for ensuring the quality of molds used in plastic manufacturing processes.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Inspect mold components (cavities, cores, inserts)  
• Validate mold performance during trials  
• Identify and resolve mold -related defects  
• Ensure compliance with quality standards  
• Maintain inspection and validation reports  
• Work with production to improve mold quality  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of plastic molding and mold construction  
• Inspection and validation techniques  
• Problem -solving and root cause analysis  
• Attention to detail  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mech anical / Plastics  
Experience  
• 3–7 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹30,000 – ₹60,000 per month
