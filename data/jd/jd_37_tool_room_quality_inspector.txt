37. Tool Room Quality Inspector

Job Summary  
Responsible for inspe cting tools, dies, molds, jigs, and fixtures to ensure they meet design 
specifications and quality standards before production use.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Inspect tooling components using measuring instruments  
• Verify dimensions against engineering drawings  
• Perform visual and functional inspections  
• Identify defects and non -conformities  
• Maintain inspection reports and records  
• Support corrective and preventive actions  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of inspection techniques and standards  
• Proficiency with measur ing tools (vernier, micrometer, gauges)  
• Basic GD&T understanding  
• Attention to detail  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 1–3 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹15,000 – ₹28,000 per month
