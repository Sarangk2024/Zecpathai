31. Tool Maintenance Technician

Job Summary  
Responsible for routine maintenance, inspection, and minor repairs of tools, dies, molds, jigs, 
and fixtures to ensure smooth production operations.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Perform rout ine inspection and maintenance of tools and dies  
• Clean, lubricate, and replace worn components  
• Assist in tool setup and alignment  
• Identify early signs of wear or damage  
• Maintain maintenance logs and records  
• Support breakdown maintenance activities  
Required Skills  
• Basic mechanical and troubleshooting skills  
• Knowledge of tooling components and assemblies  
• Familiarity with hand tools and measuring instruments  
• Attention to detail  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die / Mechanical  
Experience  
• 1–3 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹15,000 – ₹28,000 per month
