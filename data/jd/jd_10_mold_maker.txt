10. Mold Maker

Job Summary  
Responsible for manufacturing and maintaining molds used in plastic injection, blow 
molding, and related processes.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture and assemble molds  
• Perform polishing and finishi ng of mold cavities  
• Repair and maintain molds  
• Conduct mold trials and adjustments  
• Ensure cooling and ejection systems function properly  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of plastic molding processes  
• High -precision machining and polishing  
• Understanding of mol d design  
• Troubleshooting skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die / Mold Technology  
Experience  
• 4–7 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹45,000 per month
