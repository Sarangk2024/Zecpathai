21. Grinding Specialist (Tooling)

Job Summary  
Specializes in precision grinding operations to achieve tight tolerances and fine surface 
finishes on tooling components.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Operate surface, cylindrical, and to ol & cutter grinding machines  
• Achieve high -precision finishes and tolerances  
• Inspect ground components  
• Maintain grinding wheels and machines  
• Ensure quality standards are met  
Required Skills  
• Expertise in grinding techniques  
• Knowledge of tolerances and  surface finish  
• Understanding of materials and heat treatment  
• Precision measurement skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Machinist / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 3–7 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹45,000 per month
