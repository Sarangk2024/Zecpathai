70. Mold Design Consultant

Job Summary  
Provides expert consulting on mold design for plastic components, focusing on performance, 
quality, and cost efficiency.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Design and review injection/blow molds  
• Optimize cooling, gating, and ejection systems  
• Conduct mold flow analysis (if required)  
• Support mold trials and improvements  
• Improve cycle time and product quality  
• Guide mold design teams  
Required Skills  
• Mold design and plastic processing expertise  
• CAD tools (NX, Creo, SolidWorks)  
• Mold flow analysis knowledge  
• Problem -solving skills  
Qualification  
• B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Plastics  
Experience  
• 8–15+ years  
Earnings (India)  
• ₹1,500 – ₹6,000 per hour  
• ₹1,00,000 – ₹5,00,000 per project
