73. Freelance Tool Designer

Job Summary  
Works independently to de sign tools, dies, molds, jigs, and fixtures for clients across 
industries.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Create 2D drawings and 3D models  
• Design tools, dies, and molds as per client requirements  
• Modify and optimize existing designs  
• Coordinate with clients and manufacturers  
• Deliver designs within deadlines  
• Maintain design documentation  
Required Skills  
• CAD software expertise (SolidWorks, NX, CATIA, Creo)  
• Strong design and visualization skills  
• Knowledge of manufacturing processes  
• Time management and client handling  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 3–10+ years  
Earnings (India)  
• ₹500 – ₹3,000 per hour  
• ₹20,000 – ₹2,00,000 per project
