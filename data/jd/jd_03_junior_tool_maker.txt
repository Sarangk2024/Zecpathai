3. Junior Tool Maker

Job Summary  
Supports tool manufacturing and repair activities with limited supervision after initial 
training.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Assist in manufacturing dies, molds, and fixtures  
• Operat e basic machines (lathe, drilling, milling)  
• Perform fitting, polishing, and finishing tasks  
• Support tool trials and adjustments  
• Maintain records of tool components  
Required Skills  
• Basic machining and fitting skills  
• Ability to read engineering drawings   
• Knowledge of measuring tools (vernier, micrometer)  
• Problem -solving mindset  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die Making  
Experience  
• 1–2 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹12,000 – ₹20,000 per month
