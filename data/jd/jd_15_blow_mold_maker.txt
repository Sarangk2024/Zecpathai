15. Blow Mold Maker

Job Summary  
Responsible for manufacturing molds used in blow molding processes for plastic products.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture and assemble blow molds  
• Perform polishing and finishing  
• Support mold trials and production  
• Repair and maintain molds  
• Ensure dimensional accuracy  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of blow molding processes  
• Precision machining and polishing  
• Mold design understanding  
• Problem -solving ability  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool &  Die / Plastics  
Experience  
• 4–7 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹45,000 per month
