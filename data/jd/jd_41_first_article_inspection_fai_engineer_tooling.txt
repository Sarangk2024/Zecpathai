41. First Article Inspection (FAI) Engineer (Tooling)

Job Summary  
Responsible for conducting first article inspections to verify that tooling and initial 
production meet design specifications.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Perform f irst article inspections on tooling and components  
• Compare manufactured parts with design drawings  
• Prepare FAI reports and documentation  
• Identify deviations and initiate corrective actions  
• Work with production and design teams for improvements  
• Ensure compliance with customer and industry standards  
Required Skills  
• FAI process knowledge (AS9102 or equivalent standards preferred)  
• GD&T and drawing interpretation  
• Measurement and inspection expertise  
• Documentation skills  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B. Tech in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 2–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹30,000 – ₹65,000 per month
