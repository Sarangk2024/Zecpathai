23. Lathe / Milling Machine Operator (Tool Room)

Job Summary  
Operates lathe and milling machines to manufacture tooling components in a tool room 
environment.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Perform turning and milling operations  
• Prepa re machine setups and fixtures  
• Produce components as per drawings  
• Inspect parts for accuracy  
• Maintain machine and tools  
Required Skills  
• Lathe and milling machine operation  
• Knowledge of cutting tools and machining parameters  
• Drawing reading skills  
• Measurement and inspection  
Qualification  
• ITI in Machinist / Turner / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 2–4 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹18,000 – ₹30,000 per month
