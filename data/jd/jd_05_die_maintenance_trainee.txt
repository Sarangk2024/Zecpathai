5. Die Maintenance Trainee

Job Summary  
Focused on learning maintenance, repair, and troubleshooting of dies used in production 
processes.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Assist in die inspection and maintenance  
• Learn troubleshooting of  die-related issues  
• Support die assembly and disassembly  
• Perform cleaning, lubrication, and minor repairs  
• Maintain maintenance logs  
Required Skills  
• Basic mechanical troubleshooting knowledge  
• Interest in maintenance and repair work  
• Attention to detail   
• Basic tool handling skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 0–1 year  
Salary (India)  
• ₹9,000 – ₹16,000 per month
