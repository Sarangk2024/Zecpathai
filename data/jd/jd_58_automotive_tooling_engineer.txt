58. Automotive Tooling Engineer

Job Sum mary  
Designs and develops tooling solutions for automotive component manufacturing.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Develop tooling for automotive parts (sheet metal, plastic, casting)  
• Coordinate with product design and production teams  
• Ensure compliance with automotive quality standards  
• Optimize tooling for mass production  
• Support tool trials and production ramp -up  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of automotive manufacturing processes  
• Tool design and development expertise  
• Familiarity with industry standards (IATF, etc.)  
• Problem -solving and analytical skills  
Qualification  
• B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Automobile  
Experience  
• 4–8 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹40,000 – ₹90,000 per month
