24. Tool Design Engineer

Job Summary  
Designs tools, jigs, fixtures, and gauges required for manufacturing processes, ensuring 
efficiency, accuracy, and cost -effectiveness.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Design tools, fixtures, and gauges  using CAD software  
• Prepare 2D drawings and 3D models  
• Select materials and define tolerances  
• Collaborate with production and tool room teams  
• Support tool trials and modifications  
• Ensure manufacturability and cost optimization  
Required Skills  
• CAD soft ware (AutoCAD, SolidWorks, CATIA, NX)  
• Understanding of machining and manufacturing processes  
• GD&T and tolerance stack -up knowledge  
• Problem -solving and design analysis  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering  
Experience  
• 2–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹50,000 per month
