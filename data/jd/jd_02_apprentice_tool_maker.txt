2. Apprentice Tool Maker

Job Summary  
A structured training role under apprenticeship programs focusing on practical exposure to 
tool room operations.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Work under supervision to learn tool manufacturing  
• Perform basic machining and fitting tasks  
• Assist in die maintenance and repair work  
• Learn to use precision measuring instruments  
• Follow apprenticeship curriculum and assessments  
Required Skills  
• Basic mechanical aptitude  
• Discipline and eagerness to learn  
• Ability to follow instructions  
• Basic workshop safety knowledge  
Qualification  
• ITI in Tool & Die Maker / Fitter / Machinist  
Experience  
• Freshers  
Salary (India)  
• ₹7,000 – ₹12,000 per month (stipend -based)
