19. CNC Machinist (Tooling)

Job Summary  
Focuses on machining tooling components using CNC equipment with emphasis on precision 
and repeatability.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Set up and operate CNC machines  
• Execute machining operati ons for dies, molds, and fixtures  
• Monitor tool wear and replace tools as needed  
• Inspect finished components  
• Maintain production records  
Required Skills  
• CNC operation and setup  
• Understanding of machining parameters  
• Knowledge of tooling materials  
• Meas urement and inspection skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in CNC / Mechanical  
Experience  
• 2–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹20,000 – ₹40,000 per month
