17. CNC Tool Maker

Job Summary  
Responsible for manufacturing precision tooling components using CNC machines, including 
milling, turning, and multi -axis machining.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Program and operate CNC machines (VMC, H MC, CNC lathe)  
• Manufacture tooling components such as dies, molds, and fixtures  
• Interpret CAD drawings and CAM programs  
• Ensure dimensional accuracy and surface finish  
• Perform machine setup, tool selection, and offsets  
• Troubleshoot machining issues  
Required Skills  
• CNC programming (G -code, CAM software)  
• Strong understanding of tooling materials  
• Knowledge of tolerances and GD&T  
• Precision measurement skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in CNC / Tool & Die / Mechanical  
Experience  
• 3–6 years  
Salary (Ind ia) 
• ₹25,000 – ₹50,000 per month
