66. Field Service Engineer (Tooling)

Job Summary  
Provides on -site technical support, maintenance, and troubleshooting services fo r tooling at 
customer locations.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Visit customer sites for tool installation and service  
• Diagnose and resolve tooling issues  
• Perform maintenance and repairs  
• Provide technical guidance to customers  
• Maintain service records and reports  
• Ensure customer satisfaction  
Required Skills  
• Strong troubleshooting and repair skills  
• Knowledge of tooling and production processes  
• Communication and customer -handling skills  
• Willingness to travel  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 2–6 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹60,000 per month (plus travel allowances)
