49. Tool Room Supervisor

Job Summary  
Oversees daily tool room operations, ensuring efficient production, quality compliance, and 
workforce coordination.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Supervise tool room machinists, technicians, and operators  
• Assign tasks and monitor daily production activities  
• Ensure adherence to production schedules  
• Maintain quality and safety standards  
• Coordinate with design, maintenance, and production teams  
• Handle shift planning and reporting  
Required Skills  
• Strong kn owledge of tool room operations  
• Leadership and team management  
• Problem -solving and decision -making  
• Communication and coordination  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 5–8 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹30,000 – ₹60,000 per month
