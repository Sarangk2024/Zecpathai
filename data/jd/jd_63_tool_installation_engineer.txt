63. Tool Installation Engineer

Job Summary  
Responsible for installing tools, dies, molds, and fixtures at production sites, ensuring proper 
setup a nd readiness for operation.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Install and align tools, dies, and molds on machines  
• Verify setup parameters and operational readiness  
• Coordinate with production and maintenance teams  
• Conduct initial test runs and adjustments  
• Ensure safety and quality compliance  
• Provide basic operator training during installation  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of tool setup and alignment  
• Understanding of machining and production equipment  
• Troubleshooting and problem -solving skills  
• Communication  and coordination  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 2–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹50,000 per month
