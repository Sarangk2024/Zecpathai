11. Jig and Fixture Maker

Job Summary  
Designs and manufactures jigs and fixtures to support machining and assembly operations.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture jigs, fixtures, and gauges  
• Ensure proper alignment and clamping  
• Support production efficiency improvements  
• Modify existing fixtures based on requirements  
• Conduct trials and validations  
Required Skills  
• Strong understanding of machining processes  
• Fixture design basics  
• Precision measurement and alignment  
• Problem -solving ability  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 3–6 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹22,000 – ₹38,000 per month
