8. Die Maker

Job Summary  
Specializes in manufacturing and maintaining dies used in stamping, forming, and forging 
operations.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture and assemble stamping/forming dies  
• Perform die spotting and alignment  
• Repair worn or damaged die components  
• Conduct trials and adjustments  
• Ensure dimensional accuracy  
Required Skills  
• Expertise in die construction and fitting  
• Knowledge of sheet metal behavior  
• Precision machining skills   
• Problem -solving ability  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die Making  
Experience  
• 3–5 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹22,000 – ₹38,000 per month
