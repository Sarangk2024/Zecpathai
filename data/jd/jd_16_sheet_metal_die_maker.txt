16. Sheet Metal Die Maker

Job Summary  
Specializes in designing and manufacturing dies for sheet metal operations such as cutting, 
bending, and forming.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture sheet metal dies  
• Perform die setup and alignment  
• Conduct trials and adjustments  
• Troubleshoot die -related issues  
• Ensure production efficiency  
Required Skills  
• Strong knowledge of sheet metal processes  
• Die fitting and machining skills  
• Measurement and inspection expertise  
• Problem -solving abi lity  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die Making  
Experience  
• 3–6 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹22,000 – ₹40,000 per month
