32. Die Maintenance Engineer

Job Summary  
Responsible for maintaining and repairing dies used in stamping, forming, and forging 
operations to ensure production efficiency and quality.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Perform preventive and corrective maintenance on dies  
• Diagnose and troubleshoot die -related issues  
• Conduct die assembly, disassembly, and alignment  
• Ensure dimensional accuracy and surface finish  
• Coordinate with production and quality teams  
• Improve die life and reduce downtime  
Required Skills  
• Strong knowledge of die construction and sheet metal processes  
• Troubleshooting and root cause analysis  
• Precision measurement and fitting skills  
• Understanding of press operations  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B. Tech in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 3–6 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹50,000 per month
