50. Tool Room Manager

Job Summary  
Responsible for overall management of tool room operations, including production, 
maintenance, quality, and workforce management.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manage end -to-end tool room operatio ns  
• Plan production schedules and resource allocation  
• Ensure timely delivery of tools, dies, and molds  
• Control costs and optimize productivity  
• Lead and develop tool room teams  
• Implement continuous improvement initiatives  
• Ensure compliance with safety and quality standards  
Required Skills  
• Tool room management expertise  
• Leadership and strategic planning  
• Budgeting and cost control  
• Problem -solving and decision -making  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 8–15 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹50,000 – ₹1,20,000 per month
