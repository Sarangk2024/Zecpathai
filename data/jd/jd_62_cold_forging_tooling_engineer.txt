62. Cold Forging Tooling Engineer

Job Summary  
Designs and develops tooling for cold forging processes used in high -strength metal 
component manufacturing.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Design and develop cold forging dies and tools  
• Analyze material flow and deformation  
• Optimize tooling for durability and efficiency  
• Troubleshoot forging defects  
• Support production and tool trials  
Required Skills  
• Knowledge of forging processes and material behavior  
• Tool design and analysis expertise  
• Problem -solving and analytical skills  
• Understanding of heat treatment  
Qualificatio n 
• B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Production  
Experience  
• 4–9 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹40,000 – ₹90,000 per month
