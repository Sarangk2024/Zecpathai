44. Tooling Production Supervisor

Job Summary  
Supervises shop floor activities related to tooling manufacturing, ensuring productivity, 
quality, and safety.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Supervise tool room operators and machinists  
• Allocate tasks and monitor workforce performance  
• Ensure adherence to production plans  
• Maintain quality and safety standards  
• Handle shift operations and reporting  
• Coordinate with maintenance and quality teams  
Required Skills  
• Leadership and team management  
• Shop floor operations knowledge  
• Problem -solving and decision -making  
• Communication and coordination  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E in Mechanical  
Experience  
• 4–8 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹25,000 – ₹50,000 per mont h
