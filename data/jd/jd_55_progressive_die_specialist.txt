55. Progressive Die Specialist

Job Summary  
Expert in designing, manufacturing, and maintaining progressive dies used in high -volume 
sheet metal production.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Develop and maintain progressive dies  
• Optimize strip layout and die stages  
• Conduct die trials and performance tuning  
• Troubleshoot multi -stage die issues  
• Improve die life and reduce downtime  
• Collaborate with design and production teams  
Required Skills  
• Advanced knowledge of progressive die design and operation  
• Sheet metal forming expertise  
• Strong troubleshooting and analytical skills  
• Precision measurement and GD&T knowledge  
Qualification  
• Diploma / B.E / B.Tech in Mechanical / Tool & Die  
Experience  
• 6–10 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹40,000 – ₹90,000 per month
