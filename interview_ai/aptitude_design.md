# Aptitude AI Module – Zecpath

## Objective
Evaluate candidate’s logical thinking, reasoning ability, and situational judgment during HR interviews.

## Core Components

### 1. Question Engine
* Logical reasoning questions
* Situational judgment scenarios
* Role-based cognitive questions

### 2. Answer Understanding Engine
* Extract reasoning steps
* Detect structured thinking
* Identify decision-making patterns

### 3. Scoring Engine
* Logical correctness
* Clarity of reasoning
* Completeness of approach
* Decision quality

### 4. Scenario Evaluator
* Compares candidate response with ideal answer patterns
* Evaluates decision effectiveness

### 5. Output
* Aptitude score (0–100)
* Reasoning breakdown
* Strengths & gaps
