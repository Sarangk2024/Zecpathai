1. Trainee Tool & Die Maker  
Job Summary  
An entry -level role focused on learning tool and die manufacturing processes, including 
machining, fitting, assembly, and maintenance under supervision.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Assist in manufacturing tools, dies, jigs,  and fixtures  
• Learn basic machining operations (lathe, milling, grinding)  
• Support senior tool makers in assembly and fitting  
• Interpret basic engineering drawings  
• Maintain workplace safety and cleanliness  
• Participate in training programs and workshops  
Required Skills  
• Basic knowledge of mechanical engineering concepts  
• Understanding of workshop tools and measuring instruments  
• Willingness to learn and attention to detail  
• Basic drawing reading skills  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die Making, Ma chinist, or Mechanical  
Experience  
• 0–1 year  
Salary (India)  
• ₹8,000 – ₹15,000 per month

7. Tool & Die Maker  
Job Summary  
Responsible for manufacturing, assembling, repairing, and maintaining tools, dies, molds, 
jigs, and fixtures used in production processes.  
Key Responsibilities  
• Manufacture and assemble tools, dies , molds, and fixtures  
• Interpret engineering drawings and CAD models  
• Perform machining operations (lathe, milling, grinding, EDM)  
• Conduct fitting, finishing, and alignment  
• Troubleshoot and repair damaged tools  
• Support production during tool trials  
Required Skills  
• Strong machining and fitting skills  
• Drawing interpretation and GD&T basics  
• Knowledge of materials and heat treatment  
• Precision measurement (micrometer, vernier, gauges)  
Qualification  
• ITI / Diploma in Tool & Die Making / Mechanical  
Experie nce 
• 3–6 years  
Salary (India)  
• ₹20,000 – ₹40,000 per month
